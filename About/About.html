Projects


Recport

Recport is a tool to automate the enumeration process for domain names and process the report for analysis. It makes use of API's to first look for the possible subdomains for the provided domain and then filter out the live subdomains for further enumeration. Once we get live domains we make use of nmap to look for open ports on those vhosts also in end using ffuf it does directory enumeration for hidden paths. Explore More >


Drops

Drops is a plug-and-play device which incorporates IoT in its core. We created this product with an objective to give the user the ability to not only monitor the plants remotely but also water them with the push of a button over the internet. Drops has an add-on of Augmented Reality to enhance the user experience which brings the process of watering the plant in front of the user’s eyes in a fun, interactive way using 3D models and animation Explore More >
